SCIENTIFIC FOUNDATIONS

SIX MECHANISMS.
HONEST ABOUT ALL OF THEM.

Each mechanism is rated by evidence quality. No overclaiming. The machine targets well-characterised neural dynamics — and is honest where evidence is limited.

01

PHOTIC DRIVING & FFR

Visual cortex frequency-following responses entrain to disk geometry, not raw strobe. f_eff = (RPM/60) × N_arms. A 4-arm disk at 10 RPM produces 40 Hz gamma — Zheng et al. (2022) showed this entrains the amygdala, hippocampus, and insula.

EVIDENCE: ROBUST AT 40 Hz
02

KLÜVER FORM CONSTANTS

Klüver (1928): visual cortex generates four universal geometric forms under hyperactivation — tunnel, spiral, lattice, filigree. These are endogenous to neural architecture. The disk geometry directly targets all four.

EVIDENCE: WELL-ESTABLISHED
03

DMN SUPPRESSION

Default Mode Network suppression during intense sensory entrainment opens a brief neuroplasticity window. REBUS model (Carhart-Harris & Friston 2019): relaxed priors make environmental content maximally influential on neural consolidation.

EVIDENCE: STRONG (PSI); MILD (AV ONLY)
04

QRI SYMMETRY THEORY

Gómez Emilsson (QRI): mathematical symmetry of conscious experience determines hedonic valence. High-symmetry patterns — spirals, lattice, moiré, Lissajous figures — should theoretically maximise positive valence during entrainment.

EVIDENCE: THEORETICAL, UNDER VALIDATION
05

NEURAL ANNEALING

Johnson (QRI 2019): high-energy brain states flatten the neural energy landscape — creating a reconsolidation window shaped by environmental input. Beautiful geometry during peak states mechanistically shapes what consolidates.

EVIDENCE: THEORETICAL + EMPIRICAL SUPPORT
06

MOIRÉ & 3D EMERGENCE

Taylor's fractal preference research: D = 1.4–1.5 is the human visual preference sweet spot. Counter-rotating staggered disks produce both 2D moiré interference and physical 3D parallax depth cues — emergent complexity D ≈ 1.6.

EVIDENCE: ESTABLISHED (FRACTAL AESTHETICS)

THE MACHINE

THREE DISKS.
ONE ESP32.

SAFETY FRAMEWORK

HARDWARE-ENFORCED.
NO SOFTWARE OVERRIDES.

Photosensitive epilepsy affects 3–5% of the population. Every safety limit is implemented at the hardware timer interrupt level and cannot be bypassed by UI or WiFi commands.

▲ 4 Hz

STROBE HARD CAP

Danger zone for PSE: 3–30 Hz. Firmware imposes an absolute 4 Hz maximum on raw strobe via hardware interrupt. Neural entrainment at 40 Hz is achieved through disk geometry — (N × RPM / 60) — not raw strobe rate.

○ 3s

CONSENT GATE

An illuminated physical button requires a 3-second intentional hold to activate. Laminated A4 signage lists exclusion criteria: epilepsy history, photosensitive conditions, pregnancy, psychiatric medication, recent head injury.

■ STOP

EMERGENCY STOP

Simultaneous press of both rotary encoders triggers immediate motor + LED cutoff — full blackout in under 50 ms. Operators carry a wireless key fob with the same function. Post-session integration mode available.

↕ STAND

STANDING FORMAT

Intentionally lower-immersion than supine devices (Lucia No.03). Users step away, close eyes, or walk out at any moment. Default session: 10 min with automated 2-min fade-out. Trained operator required for festival deployment.
